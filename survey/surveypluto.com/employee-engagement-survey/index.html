The Gallup Q12 Model
The Gallup Q12 model includes 12 key questions that cover various aspects such as employee satisfaction with their work, sense of belonging, opportunities for development, and support from superiors. These questions can help companies identify key factors in employee engagement, thereby formulating corresponding management strategies to improve employee engagement and company performance.
The Employee Engagement Survey Index (EEI)
This model is based on the concept of the psychological contract between employees and the organization. It measures employee engagement by assessing factors such as trust, fairness, promotion, retention, effort and commitment.
