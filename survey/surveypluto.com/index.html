Market Research Model

MaxDiff

Used to understand the respondent's preference level for product attributes.

Conjoint Analysis

Used to understand the respondent's preferences for multi-attribute and multi-level products.It can help determine the optimal product features, price sensitivity, market share, etc.

KANO

Used for classifying and prioritizing user needs, reflecting the nonlinear relationship between product performance and user satisfaction.

PSM

Used to measure the satisfaction or acceptance level of users for different prices, thereby determining a suitable product price and obtaining a suitable price range, etc.

Product Concept Testing

Conduct an overall evaluation of one or more product concepts. Suitable for new product development and optimization of existing products.

Product Comparison Test

Compare several similar products, find the most popular product, and thus have a direction for product improvement and market promotion.
