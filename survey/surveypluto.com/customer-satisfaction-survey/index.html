How to accurately understand the real
thoughts of customers?
Set Dimension Indicators
By setting dimensions such as product or service quality, after-sales service, service attitude, response time, price, and environment, customer feedback can be categorized.
Subdivide Indicators Content
Based on the pre-set dimensions, edit the question content and make use of various question types, advanced logic, and other features to easily collect accurate feedback.
Increase Subjective Feedback
Provide subjective feedback questions, allowing customers to express their true thoughts. The system categorizes the subjective feedback and presents it in a word cloud display.
How are the collected responses analyzed?
Provide various customer satisfaction survey reports.
Monthly Comparative Analysis Report
Through the monthly reports, businesses can better track the changing trends of customer satisfaction survey, promptly understand customer needs and issues, and take measures to improve customer satisfaction, thereby enhancing customer loyalty. It helps businesses understand market changes and shifts in customer needs more quickly, thereby allowing timely adjustments to products, services, marketing strategies, and more.
Annual Comparative Analysis Report
By analyzing the annual customer satisfaction survey reports, enterprises can understand their long-term development trends, thus formulating more accurate long-term development strategies to better cope with market competition.
Comparison Report for Various Regions
By comparing customer satisfaction survey levels across different regions, businesses can gain insights into their performance and identify potential issues in the eyes of their customers in each region. If customer satisfaction survey is low in a particular region, it may indicate that there are problems with the products or services offered there, prompting the company to make targeted optimizations. This allows businesses to better understand the demands and preferences of different local markets, enabling them to achieve precise market positioning.
Customer Satisfaction Survey Detailed Report
Through customer satisfaction survey reports, businesses can gain a better understanding of their customers' needs and preferences, allowing them to provide more satisfactory products and services. By improving areas that customers are dissatisfied with, businesses can enhance customer loyalty, increase competitiveness, improve customer retention rates, and promote company growth.
Comparative Analysis of Various Types of Customers
Based on customer satisfaction survey data comparisons in terms of age, gender, region, education, income, etc., understand the differences in satisfaction among different customer groups towards the product, establish user personas for the product. Find the most suitable marketing targets for the product.
Professional SWOT Analysis
Conducting a professional SWOT analysis on the NPS value and various dimensions of customer satisfaction survey can help understand the fundamental reasons that influence customer preference and recommendations. This allows clear identification of the strengths and weaknesses of a business, enabling targeted improvements and optimizations to drive increased business revenue.
