Changing the Survey Title

﻿Changing the Survey Title


You have the flexibility to change your survey title anytime. Follow these steps to make it:


1. Access the survey you want to edit.


2. Click on the Build or Edit Survey button to access the survey editor.




3. Select the survey title and enter the new survey title.




4. Click Save in the top right corner to save this survey.


With these steps, you can quickly and easily update your survey title to better reflect your survey's content and purpose.



Survey Title Settings


To change the color and font size of your survey title, follow these simple steps:


1. Click Settings Home and Select Style to access survey settings.





2. Under Color Matching, click Buttons & Title to change the color of your survey title and buttons. You can also change the overall theme to adjust the color of the title, buttons, and background.




3. Change font size by choosing from two Font Size options: Standard or Larger.




4. Click Save to save your changes. 




Once you make changes, the live survey preview on the right side of the page will reflect the updates immediately. With this feature, you can customize your survey to suit your needs and preferences.



Hiding the Survey Title


If you prefer not to display the survey title to your respondents, follow these simple steps:


1. Click Settings Home and select Style to access survey settings.


2. Toggle the Hide Survey Title option to turn it on or off.




3. Click Save to apply your changes.
